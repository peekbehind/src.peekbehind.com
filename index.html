About

PeekBehind shows the mouse cursors and key presses of your users as ghosts layered on top of each page of your web site.

This visualization, inspired from video games, is a modern and efficient way to gain insights in the patterns of use of interactive applications.

As stated by usability expert Jakob Nielsen, as little as 5 users are sufficient to identify the strength and shortcomings of a user interface.

With PeekBehind, as soon as you navigate to a page of your own web site, you see a replay of the activity recorded for the 5 most recent visitors of the page, shown simultaneously.

PeekBehind respects the privacy of your visitors and records nothing when the "Do Not Track" preference of the browser is checked. You can take advantage of this exception to avoid recording the activity of yourself and your own employees.
